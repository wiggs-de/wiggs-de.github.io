Hi! I'm Dev. I've got many plates spinning. I'm based out of the riprarian forests of the Southeastern United States, with my family.
I am an artist working with/thru visible, audible, and spatial mediums. I specialize in experimental photographic/sound processes and small-scale installations.
My art deals with ecologies and perspectives, relationships and attitudes really, and the theories of non-site and simulacra. I have a modest tool-set that includes field recording, biodata/geodata sonification, photography, sketching, and cartography.
I am an indepedent researcher working under the umbrella-organization of the Institute for Post-Anthropocentric Ecology. The IFPAE is an art-research group I founded, in the winter of '22, that is focused on publishing and outreach.
I operate a (very) small bookstore and design agency (landscape, interior, graphic) under the name of Written-Material.
I publish a weekly art and design newsletter under the name of ART/CLASS.
