dev wiggers

I'm an interdisciplinary artist-researcher-designer, working between the bioregions of the colorado rockies forests and the southeast savannas and riparian forests. My work is closely tied to the land, and the ecological relationships that exist between the earth and life.


LENS-BASED

Digital, analog, and experimental photographic and video processes


SOUND-BASED

Field recording, biodata sonification, soil-sounding, experimental composition, and audio-place-mapping


MATERIAL-BASED

Wood-sculpture, concrete-sculpture, and lost-wax casting processes


DESIGN-BASED

Thoughtful, small-scale structures, objects, and landscapes, that are focused on post-anthropocentric guidelines
